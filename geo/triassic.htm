When was it?

245 to 208 million years ago.

What was going on?

Dinosaurs first appear.

What were the continents like?

Pangaea. One BIG continent.

What kind of life existed?

Dinosaurs evolve from the Thecodonts (an earlier dinosaur-like type of reptile); the first mammals appear, although they are small and marginal.
