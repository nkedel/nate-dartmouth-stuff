The Holocene Epoch

When was it?

40,000 years ago to present.

What was going on?

The Neandertals die out in Europe; Modern humans spread across the globe.

What were the continents like?

Pretty much modern.

What kind of life existed?

Neandertals finish disappearing in Europe around 30-35 KYA; modern humans cross land bridge into the Americas sometime between 35 and 17 KYA. Agriculture is invented in the old world around 10 KYA and in the Americas around 4.5 KYA. Modern civilizations emerge in Egypt and the Near East around 6 kya. Alexander the Great - 2.5 KYA. Roman Empire at its height, Christianity founded 2 KYA. Medieval civilization at its height - 1 KYA. Columbus "discovers" America - 0.5 kya. Industrial revolution begins - 0.2 KYA (1790s in England).
