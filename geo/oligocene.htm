The Oligocene Epoch

Continue the tour to the Miocene

When was it?

37 to 24 million years ago.

What was going on?

Mammals and birds continue to diversify. The first Anthropoids appear.

What were the continents like?

Things look pretty modern, but a lot of the lower areas are under water. Africa bumpes into Eurasia, but water covers much of Europe.

What kind of life existed?

Mammals and birds continue to diversify and to more closely resemble the modern forms. The first Anthropoid primates, like Aegyptopithecus, appear; these may be ancestral to modern Apes, but are less derived than either modern apes or monkeys.
