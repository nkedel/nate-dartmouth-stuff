The Eocene Epoch

Continue the tour to the Oligocene

When was it?

58 to 37 million years ago.

What was going on?

Mammals and birds continue to diversify. The first prosimians appear.

What were the continents like?

Things look pretty modern, but a lot of the lower areas are under water. South and North America have not yet connected, nor has Africa bumped into Eurasia. The Bering land bridge between Eurasia and North America is open.

What kind of life existed?

Mammals and birds continue to fill the niches left open by the departed Dinos. The cetaceans (whales) appear. The first prosimians appear, which are the first group of primates which still exist today.
