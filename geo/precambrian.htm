The precambrian is a term used for the time prior to the current Eon; it is not a formal designation, but rather covers three Eons of time prior to the current one. It covers a period of roughly 4 billion years from the formation of the earth through the start of the Cambrian Era. (Stanley, Earth and Life through Time, ch. 9)
